FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before making the switch to solar.

How long does a solar installation take? ▼
Most residential installations are completed in 1 to 3 days. Commercial and agricultural systems may take 1–2 weeks depending on the scale. We handle all permits and grid connection paperwork, which can add 2–6 weeks before your system is officially commissioned and generating power.
Will solar panels work on cloudy or rainy days? ▼
Yes! Modern solar panels can generate electricity even in overcast or cloudy conditions, though output will be reduced compared to full sun. On average, panels produce 10–25% of their rated capacity on cloudy days. Pairing with a battery storage system ensures you always have power available regardless of weather.
What warranties come with my solar system? ▼
We provide a 10-year workmanship warranty on all installations. Solar panels come with a 25-year performance guarantee from the manufacturer. Inverters are covered for 10–12 years, and battery storage systems carry a 10-year warranty. We also offer an optional extended service plan for comprehensive ongoing maintenance coverage.
What financing options are available? ▼
We offer several flexible options: outright purchase (lowest total cost), bank EMI loans through partner banks with low interest rates, and government-backed financing under PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana. Our team will help you identify and apply for the best subsidy and loan combination for your situation.
How do I claim the government solar subsidy? ▼
Under the PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana, eligible residential customers can receive subsidies up to ₹78,000 for a 3 kW system. We handle the entire subsidy application process on your behalf — from portal registration to DISCOM approval — so you don't need to do anything extra.
Will solar damage my roof? ▼
When installed correctly by certified professionals, solar panels actually protect your roof from UV degradation, rain, and debris. Our team conducts a roof integrity assessment before installation. If your roof needs repairs or reinforcement, we'll advise you before proceeding to ensure a safe, watertight installation that preserves your roof's lifespan.
